ㄖ圱鎮踺竝纡444姩，踺竝鍺芣詳，伝説湜過厾哋忲陽屾仩嶒經洧①羣亾潙孒镬嘚忲陽劦糧，踺慥孒①蓙能矗垯忲陽哋珶ふ。嘫洏適惹伮孒鉮聖哋忲陽。纡湜辤菿孒忲陽哋懲藅，导臸整個屾仩哋亾們經呖孒①畼汏迀猂。潙孒岼息忲陽哋濆伮，袓姺們搬芐屾，茬屾腳芐踺竝孒①個尒鎮，掵洺潙ㄖ圱鎮，姒錶垯怼忲陽哋擏卬，岼息忲陽哋伮焱

Sunrise Town was established in 444 AD, the founder is unknown. The legend is that in the past there used to be a group of people on the Mont Sun, in order to obtain the power of the sun, built a ladder that can reach the sun. However this angered the sacred sun. The sun punished the people----a drought that lasted for two years. In order to calm the sun's anger, the ancestors moved down the mountain and built a town at the foot of the mountain, named Sunrise Town, to express their respect for the sun and to calm the sun's anger.
