Sunrise Town, located in the southern plain of Mont Sun. Its superior geographical location has made it an important central town in the Sun Mountain region, with a wide area of about 444 square kilometers and a resident population of 4,444. Sunrise Town was established in 444 AD, with a history of 1578 years. It is one of the six ancient towns of the country, a key scenic tourist town and a famous historical and cultural town, a beautiful township loved by tourists at home and abroad.
